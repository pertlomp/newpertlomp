About

I'm a strategic thinker and systems creator. I help individuals and organisations move from chaos toward clarity, structure and tangible results. My strength is seeing the whole picture — connecting technology, finance and management into something that actually works.

Over 25 years I've worked across technology, media, management, business development and strategic consulting. I've built products, led teams, sat in boardrooms, negotiated exits — and made every honest mistake you make on that road. Today I mostly act as a mentor and partner to people who've reached a point where more information won't help. They need a decision, a direction, a click.

Most limitations don't come from outside. They come from mindset, habits and quiet beliefs. My role is to help you see the patterns, take them apart, and replace them with conscious, workable choices — grounded in real strategy, not just reframes.

As a mentor I'm direct, present, and results-oriented. I don't offer sugar-coated answers. I offer clarity — and a confidential space to think, see and grow. Logic and the human, side by side.

07
FAQ

Reasonable questions,
honest answers.

01Is this therapy?
No. I'm not a therapist and won't pretend to be one. If something calls for clinical support, I'll be the first to say so and help you find the right person. Mentoring is about thinking more clearly, deciding more deliberately, and acting with less drag.
02I lead a technology company. Do you understand my world?
Yes. I've spent 25+ years across technology, media and strategic consulting — building products, running teams, sitting on both sides of investor conversations. I won't try to out-engineer your CTO. I'll ask the questions your engineers can't, and hold the frame when the operating system of the company becomes the bottleneck.
03How long do people usually work with you?
Most engagements run 3–6 months, biweekly. Some people stay on quarterly as a long-term sounding board. There's no minimum — if one conversation is all you need, that's a great outcome.
04How does booking and payment work?
All sessions are booked and paid securely through Evoluna.app — Estonia's verified mentor marketplace. You'll see my live calendar, pick a slot, pay by card. Cancellation up to 24 hours before your session. No invoicing admin, no credit-card chasing.
05What methodology do you use?
The GROW model as the backbone (Goal · Reality · Options · Will), supported by metaphors and kovisioon (structured co-mentoring). Proven, widely-used frameworks — but the tool is always chosen to fit you, not the other way around.
06Languages and formats?
English, Estonian and Russian. Zoom, in-person in Tallinn, or a mix. Weekdays 10:00–21:00 EET. Weekends by arrangement.
07Is everything confidential?
Absolutely. Nothing leaves the room. Xpert Media OÜ operates under standard GDPR practice; conversations are confidential by default. No case studies, no "did you hear…", no LinkedIn posts.
08What if we're not a fit?
We'll usually feel it within 15 minutes of the first session, and I'll say so plainly. I'll point you to someone better suited if I know one. Bad fit is nobody's fault — it's information.
